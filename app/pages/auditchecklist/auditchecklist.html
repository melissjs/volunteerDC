Audit Checklist Preparation Checklist Read the Instructions and FAQs pages before election day. Bring your fully charged cell phone, water, sunscreen, sunglasses, a jacket, money for parking and food if needed and a battery charger for your phone if you will be at the polling location for a long time. If your polling location has an outlet, a regular phone charger may suffice. Remember not to wear or bring any clothing or other items that promote or disparage any specific candidate or party and remain unbiased in your interactions with voters. Wear a solid color shirt, preferably black so that you and your team members may be identified more easily to voters. During Audit Checklist Carefully follow the morning check in and evening check out procedures as a team on election day. Watch all entrances and exits and do your best to get as close to 100% of all exiting voters as possible. Train new incoming team members that need help logging in or understanding the functions of the app. Inform each other if you are witnessing anomalous activities at your location. Be kind and polite to your team member and voters throughout the day and have fun.
